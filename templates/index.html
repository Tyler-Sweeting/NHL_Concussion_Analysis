NHL Concussion Analysis

Concussions are a significant concern in the National Hockey League (NHL), as they can have serious and long-lasting effects on players' health and well-being. The league has implemented various measures to address the issue, including the introduction of a concussion protocol that requires players to undergo a series of tests before being cleared to return to play. Additionally, the NHL has implemented stricter penalties for hits to the head and has increased the use of video review to ensure that dangerous plays are penalized. Despite these efforts, there is still much work to be done to reduce the incidence of concussions in the NHL and to better understand the long-term effects of these injuries on players.

In our project we develop a Machine Learning models that predict the impact a concussion will have on a players future performance after the concussion
